Let's get your screen set up

Two short steps and Mum will have today's plan up on the wall — no technical experience needed, and everything is managed from your phone or computer.

1

Set up the viewer screen

On the tablet or display itself:

  1. Plug it in and connect it to your home Wi‑Fi, as you would with any tablet.
  2. Open a web browser and go to dailyview.org/display
  3. Add it to the home screen (share/menu button → Add to Home Screen), so it reopens automatically if the screen restarts.
Open the viewer setup page →
2

Sign in to your dashboard

On your own phone or computer, not the screen:

Sign in to your dashboard →
3

Pair the two together

  1. In your dashboard, go to Devices → Add device and give it a name (e.g. "Mum's kitchen screen").
  2. You'll see a short code and a QR code — it's valid for 15 minutes.
  3. Back on the screen, type the code into the box shown there and select Connect.

That's it — the screen now shows today's plan and keeps itself up to date automatically. Find it a stable, plugged-in spot where it's easy to glance at.
