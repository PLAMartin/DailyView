Subject: Setting up your Daily View screen 

Hi [Name],

There are three steps to get your parent setup and Mum will have today's plan up on the wall — no
technical experience needed, and everything is managed from your phone
or computer.

View this guide as a webpage: https://www.dailyview.org/screen-setup

1) SET UP THE VIEWER SCREEN
On the tablet (or other display) itself:
  - Plug it in and connect it to your home Wi-Fi as you would with
    any tablet.
  - Open a web browser and go to: https://www.dailyview.org/display
  - Add it to the home screen (share/menu button -> "Add to Home
    Screen") so it reopens automatically if the screen restarts.

2) SIGN IN TO YOUR DASHBOARD
On your own phone or computer (not the viewer screen):
  - Sign in here: https://www.dailyview.org/login/

3) PAIR THE TWO TOGETHER
  - In your dashboard, go to Devices -> Add device and give it a name
    (e.g. "Mum's kitchen screen").
  - You'll see a short code and a QR code which is valid for 15 minutes.
  - Back on the viewer screen, type the code into the box shown there and
    select Connect.

The viewer screen now shows today's plan and keeps itself up to
date automatically. Find it a stable, plugged-in spot where it's easy
to glance at.

GETTING STARTED WITH YOUR DASHBOARD
  Today     - See what the viewer screen is showing (max 60 secs delay).
  Calendar  - Add, edit or remove events for any day.
  People    - Invite other family members or carers to help manage
              the plan.
  Devices   - Pair new viewer screens or check that an existing one is
              online.
  Messages  - Send a short note that appears on the screen.
  Settings  - Adjust reminders and other preferences.

Once it's paired, the person using the viewer screen never needs to touch a
setting or log in; everything after this is managed from your
dashboard.

Any trouble along the way, please reply to this email and I'll help
you sort it out.
